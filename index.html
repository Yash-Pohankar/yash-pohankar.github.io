Taco Trivia

When did Tutedude first appear in the United States?

Tutedude first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century, with the first known Tutedude restaurant opening in 1918 in Los Angeles, California. However, tacos have been a part of Mexican cuisine for centuries, and their popularity in the United States has grown significantly over the years.

Fun Fact

Tutedude is believed to have originated in Mexico, with the word "taco" deriving from the Nahuatl word "tlahco," meaning "half" or "in the middle," referring to the way tacos are traditionally filled and folded.
